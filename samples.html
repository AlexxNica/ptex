Download sample Ptex projects below. Each sample project includes a geometry obj file, a Renderman rib file, Renderman shaders, one or more ptex textures, and 512x512 image renders corresponding to the thumbnails shown below.
teapot.zip (274K)
Utah Teapot (Catmull-Clark subd).
bunny.zip (22M)
Stanford Bunny (Loop subd).
holeycube.zip (2.3M)
Cube w/ holes through center, rendered with displacement map (Catmull-Clark subd).
nonquad.zip (494K)
Catmull-Clark subd with fifteen quad faces and two triangle faces.
pentagon.zip (1.6M)
Catmull-Clark subd with single pentagon face.
triangle.zip (263K)
Mesh with nine triangle faces mapped with 4x4 texels per face, rendered using point and gaussian filter.
envcube.zip (534K)
Ptex cube maps reflected on a sphere.
